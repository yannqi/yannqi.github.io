I am currently a Ph.D. student at the [Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA)](http://www.ia.cas.cn/), under the guidance of [Prof. Shiming Xiang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=0ggsACEAAAAJ). I received my bachelor's degree from [University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC)](https://www.uestc.edu.cn/) under the supervision of Prof. Lu Yang.

My research focuses on building intelligent multimodal systems. I am particularly interested in:

- **Multimodal Large Language Models** -- reasoning, auto-thinking, and retrieval-augmented generation
- **Audio-Visual Learning** -- segmentation, generation, and cross-modal understanding
- **Visual Semantic Segmentation** -- open-vocabulary, continual, and remote-sensing segmentation
- **AIGC** -- image, audio, and video generation

I am happy to cooperate and share. If you are interested in my research, please feel free to contact me via [email](mailto:yangqi2021@ia.ac.cn).
